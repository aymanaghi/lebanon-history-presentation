## 1. Post-Civil War Reconstruction
- Lebanon began rebuilding cities, especially Beirut.
- **Solidere project** reconstructed downtown Beirut.
- Political system based on the **National Pact** continued.

## 2. Syrian Presence
- Syrian military and political influence remained until 2005.
- 2005: **Cedar Revolution** forced Syrian troops to leave Lebanon.

## 3. Conflicts & Wars
- 2006: Lebanon-Israel War caused major damage in southern Lebanon.
- Ongoing border tensions and occasional clashes.

## 4. Political & Economic Crises
- Sectarian politics remain deeply rooted.
- Chronic economic instability and high unemployment.
- 2019: Massive protests for political reform (**October Revolution**).
- 2020: Beirut port explosion caused massive destruction.

## 5. Modern Lebanon Today
- Diverse religious communities co-exist.
- Cultural, historical, and economic importance continues in the region.
- Challenges: political gridlock, economic recovery, social unity.
