# Independence of Lebanon (1943)

## 1. Background
- Lebanon had been under French control since 1920.
- Lebanese leaders wanted full sovereignty.
- Nationalism grew after WWII started in 1939.

## 2. Key Leaders
- **Bechara El Khoury** (President)  
- **Riad Al Solh** (Prime Minister)

## 3. National Pact
- An agreement to share power between Christians and Muslims.
- Maronite president, Sunni prime minister, Shi’a speaker of parliament.
- Laid the foundation for Lebanon’s sectarian-based political system.

## 4. 1943 Crisis
- France arrested Lebanese leaders in November 1943.
- Massive protests and international pressure forced their release.
- November 22 is celebrated as **Independence Day**.

## 5. After Independence
- Lebanon became fully sovereign.
- Began to build modern state institutions.
- Maintained a delicate balance between its religious communities.
